Tsunami

About

A series of ocean waves caused by earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, or underwater landslides. Can cause devastating coastal flooding.

Warning Signs

  • Sudden water recession
  • Unusual ocean behavior
  • Strong earthquake near coast
  • Loud ocean roar

Safety Steps

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Follow evacuation routes
  • Stay away from the coast
  • Wait for official all-clear

Earthquake

About

Sudden ground shaking caused by rock movements in the Earth's crust. Can cause structural damage and secondary hazards.

Warning Signs

  • Ground tremors
  • Objects swaying
  • Loud rumbling sounds
  • Animals acting strangely

Safety Steps

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On
  • Stay away from windows
  • If outside, find open space
  • Be prepared for aftershocks

Flood

About

Overflow of water onto normally dry land. Can be caused by heavy rainfall, storm surge, or dam failures.

Warning Signs

  • Heavy rainfall
  • Rising water levels
  • Flood warnings
  • Water entering buildings

Safety Steps

  • Move to higher ground
  • Avoid walking in water
  • Don't drive in floods
  • Follow evacuation orders
